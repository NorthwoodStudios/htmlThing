Horizon Discord Bot

About Horizon Bot

Horizon is a powerful and versatile Discord bot designed to improve your server experience with a wide range of features, from moderation tools to interactive commands.

  • Shift Management: Manage your shift with commands or on the dashboard
  • ERLC loggin: Log's from ingame to your discord server
  • Custom Commands: Make your own commands to use inside of your server(Coming Soon)
  • Moderation: ban, kick, mute commands with role management
  • Welcome messages with customizable templates
  • Music playback support with playlists and queues
  • Reaction roles and giveaways to boost engagement
  • Advanced logging and analytics to monitor your server
