Horizon Bot - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2025
Last Updated: June 26, 2025

1. What We Collect

  • Discord User IDs and Server IDs
  • Command usage and moderation logs (e.g., warns, bans, notes)
  • OAuth2 login info (basic Discord profile, guild list)

2. Why We Collect It

  • To deliver core moderation features
  • To display logs and data on the Dashboard for authorized staff
  • To prevent abuse and maintain security

3. How We Store It

All data is securely stored and access is limited to authorized systems only. Data is retained only as long as necessary for the bot to function properly in your server.

4. Who Can See It

  • Server moderators and admins (with permission)
  • Bot developers (only for troubleshooting)
  • No data is ever sold or shared with third parties

5. Your Rights

  • You can request removal of your server’s data at any time
  • You may disconnect Horizon from your server at any time

6. Children’s Privacy

Horizon is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes

We may revise this Privacy Policy when needed. You’ll always find the latest version here.
